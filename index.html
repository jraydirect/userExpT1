Getting There

By Air: Most visitors arrive via our small airport that accommodates small jets and propeller planes. Larger jets will be supported soon!

By Sea: A small cruise ship docks at Yellow Leaf Bay once per week.

Airport Expansion: Currently expanding to accommodate larger aircraft within the next few years.

Transportation

Public Buses: Serve Taniti City from 5 AM to 11 PM daily

Private Buses: Service the rest of the island

Taxis: Available in Taniti City

Rental Cars: Available near the airport

Bikes: Rentals available (helmets required by law)
